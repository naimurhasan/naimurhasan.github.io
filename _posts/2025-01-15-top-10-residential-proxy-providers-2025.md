## Technical Differentiators Explained

### Protocol Support: SOCKS5 with UDP

Most providers support HTTP(S) and SOCKS5 (TCP). However, **UDP support** is rare and critical for:
- Real-time communication (VoIP, video streaming)
- Gaming applications
- Low-latency requirements
- Non-TCP protocols

**Only Decodo explicitly supports SOCKS5 UDP**, giving them a unique technical advantage.

### Static vs Rotating Proxies

**Rotating Proxies:**
- New IP per request or time interval
- Maximum anonymity
- Prevents rate limiting
- Best for: High-volume scraping

**Static (ISP) Proxies:**
- Same IP for extended duration
- Session stability
- Trust + speed hybrid
- Best for: Account management, checkouts

### Geo-Targeting Granularity

| Level | Providers | Use Cases |
|-------|-----------|-----------|
| **Country/State** | All providers | Basic localization |
| **City** | Bright Data, Oxylabs, Decodo, SOAX | Regional pricing, local ads |
| **ZIP Code** | Bright Data | Hyper-local testing |
| **ASN/ISP** | Bright Data, Decodo | Network emulation, carrier-specific testing |
