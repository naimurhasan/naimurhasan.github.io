Our Solution

We install our MOTILIX™ Device in your car in less than 5 minutes. The device works with any vehicle, including cars, buses, light and heavy trucks.

The vehicle telematics are relayed every 2 minutes to our platform that hosts various vehicle specific predictive models. Our fault finding models are built using the latest technologies in machine learning. This allows us to deliver instantaneous alerts when anything abnormal is detected.
